About FreshFade Studio

"It's nicer when you get out."

FreshFade Studio was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to walk out of the barbershop feeling like the best version of themselves. Based in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, we bring precision, creativity, and a welcoming atmosphere to every single cut.

Whether you're here for a classic taper fade, a bold mohawk, or intricate cornrows — we approach every hairstyle with the same level of care and craftsmanship. Our online booking system makes it easy to reserve your slot, pick your style, and choose how you'd like to pay.

What We Offer

With 29+ hairstyles on our menu, from clean fades and crew cuts to braids and dreadlocks, we cater to every look. Every session is a 45-minute experience designed to give you the attention you deserve.
